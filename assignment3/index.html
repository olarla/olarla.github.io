Welcome, Idol Manager!

Your mission is to guide a trainee from their first steps to stardom! Choose activities wisely to manage Stamina and earn Experience Points (EXP) to level up your idol. With each level-up, the idol’s outfit changes, reflecting their growing status!

  • 1. Key Stats
    - Stamina: Decreases with each activity. Rest if stamina reaches 0.
    - EXP: Earn through activities to level up your idol. At 100 EXP, your idol levels up.
  • 2. Activities
    Various activities impact stats:
    - Dance Practice: -20 Stamina, +15 EXP
    - TV Appearance: -15 Stamina, +15 EXP
    - Recording Session: -10 Stamina, +20 EXP
    - Concert Performance: -25 Stamina, +25 EXP
  • 3. Resting
    Low on stamina? Use the Rest button to restore +60 Stamina.
  • 4. Leveling Up
    At 100 EXP, your idol levels up, refreshing their appearance.
  • 5. Goal
    Reach Level 4 to complete the game. Maintain stamina and gain EXP wisely to succeed!
