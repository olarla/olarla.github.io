Welcome, Idol Manager!

Your mission is to guide a trainee from their first steps to stardom! Choose activities wisely to manage Stamina and earn Experience Points (EXP) to level up your idol. With each level-up, the idol’s outfit changes, reflecting their growing status!

  • 1. Key Stats
    -Stamina: Decreases with each activity. If stamina reaches 0, let your idol rest to recover.
    -EXP: Gain EXP through activities to level up. When EXP reaches 100, your idol levels up.
  • 2. Activities
    Choose from various activities to shape your idol’s career and manage their stats:
    -Dance Practice: -20 Stamina, +15 EXP
    -TV Appearance: -15 Stamina, +15 EXP
    -Recording Session: -10 Stamina, +20 EXP
    -Concert Performance: -25 Stamina, +25 EXP
  • 3. Resting
    Is stamina low? Click the Rest button to recover +50 Stamina.
  • 4. Leveling Up
    When EXP hits 100, your idol levels up. Each level brings a fresh look!
  • 5. Goal
    Help your idol reach Level 4! Use strategies to maintain stamina and maximize EXP.
